## ⚡️ Core Philosophy: The "Validate & Offer" Framework

We do **not** sell a list. We sell a **data quality moat**.

Every outreach must:

1. **Validate a pain point** the prospect already feels (bounced emails, dirty data, wasted ad spend).
2. **Offer proof** — not a pitch — via a free 50-lead sample CSV that speaks for itself.
3. **Disappear fast.** Under 100 words. Respect their time.

Our unique differentiator **must** be mentioned in every email:

- ✅ **AI Confidence Scoring** (0–100) per email — no other vendor exposes this.
- ✅ **MX Verification** — every domain is live-checked before delivery.

## 🔍 Pre-Flight Research Protocol (5–7 Minutes Per Prospect)

Before drafting any email, invest exactly **5–7 minutes** in deep personalization. This is non-negotiable. A generic email destroys the premium positioning.

### Step-by-Step Research Checklist

| #   | Task                                              | Where to Look                                                                                               | Target Output                                                           |
| --- | ------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1   | **Identify the Founder/Owner**                    | LinkedIn Sales Navigator, agency website `/about` page, or `site:[agency.com] "founder" OR "CEO"` in Google | `[First Name]`                                                          |
| 2   | **Identify their Micro-Niche**                    | Their website's case studies, homepage headline, or client logos                                            | `[Micro-Niche]` (e.g., "MedSpa marketing", "HVAC lead gen")             |
| 3   | **Confirm they run cold email outreach**          | Their services page lists "cold email", "outbound SDR", or "lead generation"                                | ✅ / ❌                                                                 |
| 4   | **Find a specific, recent result they've shared** | LinkedIn posts, their blog, or Twitter/X in last 90 days                                                    | `[Recent Win]` (e.g., "your post about closing 3 dental clients in Q4") |
| 5   | **Estimate their current data source**            | Look for Apollo, ZoomInfo, or LeadFuze mentions in their content                                            | `[Their Current Tool]` (if known)                                       |

### ⏱ Time Budget

| Task                          | Time            |
| ----------------------------- | --------------- |
| LinkedIn + website scan       | 2–3 min         |
| Recent post / win scan        | 1–2 min         |
| Email draft & personalization | 1–2 min         |
| **Total**                     | **5–7 min max** |

> **Rule:** If you cannot find the Founder name after 3 minutes of searching → apply the Fallback Rule below. Do **not** guess.

## 🚨 Fallback Rule: Hidden Founder Name

If the Founder/Owner name is **not publicly discoverable** (no LinkedIn profile, no "About" page, anonymous brand), use `Team at [Agency Name]` as the salutation. Never use "Hey there", "Hi Friend", or leave it blank.

**Example:**

> `Hi Team at Outbound Pros,`

This preserves professionalism without hallucinating a name. If the agency is clearly solo-operated but nameless, default to `Hi [Agency Name] team,`.

## 📧 Template 1: The Bounce Rate Angle

**Best Used For:** Cold email agencies, outbound SDR agencies, done-for-you lead gen services.  
**Core Hook:** Their biggest operational risk is high bounce rates tanking deliverability.

---

**Subject Lines (A/B Test):**

- `[First Name] — what's your list bounce rate?`
- `re: [Micro-Niche] data quality`
- `[Agency Name] — your bounces might be a data problem`

---

**Email Body:**

```
Hi [First Name],

Quick one — if you're running cold email for [Micro-Niche] clients,
bounce rate is probably your #1 deliverability killer.

We build AI-verified contact lists specifically for agencies.
Every email comes with an AI confidence score (0–100) and live
MX verification — so you know exactly what you're sending before
you send it.

I'd like to send you 50 [Micro-Niche] leads free. No strings.
Just reply "yes" and I'll drop the CSV in this thread.

— [Your First Name]
```

**Word Count:** ~75 words ✅

---

**Follow-Up (Day 3, if no reply):**

```
Hi [First Name],

Just bumping this up — happy to send the 50 free [Micro-Niche]
leads whenever you're ready.

One thing I'll mention: our data includes email confidence scores
that most tools don't expose. Let me know if you'd want a look.

— [Your First Name]
```

**Word Count:** ~45 words ✅

## ✅ Pre-Send Quality Gate

Before hitting send on any email, verify:

- [ ] Email is **under 100 words** (use wordcounter.net)
- [ ] `[First Name]` and `[Micro-Niche]` placeholders are **filled in** — no raw brackets in the final email
- [ ] Subject line is **lowercase** (performs better in deliverability tests)
- [ ] The **free 50-lead CSV** offer is present
- [ ] The **AI Confidence Score** and/or **MX Verification** is mentioned at least once
- [ ] Fallback rule applied if founder name was not found
- [ ] Email is sent from a **properly warmed domain** (not your main domain)
